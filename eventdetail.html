Categories & Distances

The Kahf X Fitness Run features two exciting categories designed to cater to all fitness levels:

  • 5KM Run: Perfect for beginners and casual runners, this distance allows you to enjoy the thrill of running while soaking in the beautiful surroundings. It's a great way to kickstart your fitness journey!
  • 8KM Run: For those seeking a more competitive experience, this category challenges you to push your limits and achieve your personal best. Join fellow enthusiasts and feel the adrenaline rush as you race towards the finish line!
