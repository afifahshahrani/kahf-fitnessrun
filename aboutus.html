What is Kahf?

Kahf is a personal care company that offers halal and natural products intended exclusively for men. Our products use high-quality ingredients to provide the best treatment for your skin and hair. As part of PT Paragon Technology and Innovation, Indonesia's leading beauty company, Kahf is introducing advancements that address today's demands.

Kahf X Fitness Run

The Kahf X Fitness Run is an exciting and dynamic event that encourages individuals of all ages and backgrounds to engage in physical activity and embrace a healthier lifestyle. This event is thoughtfully designed to accommodate participants of all fitness levels, from beginners who are just starting their fitness journey to seasoned athletes looking to challenge themselves. By fostering an inclusive atmosphere , the Kahf X Fitness Run aims to inspire a sense of community and camaraderie among participants, promoting not only personal health and well-being but also the joy of shared experiences.

Kahf Vision

We aim to inspire men to lead healthier lives by making personal care and fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Kahf Mission

Kahf's mission is to provide halal-certified, environmentally-friendly skincare products designed for modern men, promoting confidence and self-care through innovative and accessible solutions. We focus on enhancing skin health while adhering to Islamic principles of purity and sustainability.
