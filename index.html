Requirements

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all participants, please take note of the following requirements:

  • Age Requirement: Participants must be 18 years or older to join the race. This ensures that everyone is ready for the challenge!
  • Arrival Time: All participants are required to arrive at the venue 30 minutes before the start time. This allows for check-in and warm-up, setting you up for success!
  • Schedule Modifications: The event organizers reserve the right to modify the event schedule if necessary. Stay tuned for any updates!
  • No Refunds: Please note that refunds are not permitted once registration is completed. Make sure you’re ready to run!
